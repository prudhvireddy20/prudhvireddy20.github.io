What I Build

I'm a B.Tech AI & ML graduate (G Pulla Reddy Engineering College, 2026) with a research internship at IIT Madras, where I built an automated vulnerability detection pipeline that fused Semgrep, CodeQL, and LLM-based reasoning to contextualize security findings and reduce false positives at scale.
